Smart Village

The 3 Pillar

To provide electricity, knowledge about new digital trends through STEM lectures to make villagers self-reliant,a step towards making 'ATMANIRBHAR BHARAT'.

Focus

To provide sustainability and networking to the target site/selected site along with clean energy solution for power outage issue and to fetch GSM Network.

Proposed Technologies

From Solar Panels for renewable energy to vermicomposting based on IOT to smart farming kits.. We are determined to implement knowledge for the society.

Ongoing Phase

There are 4 phases.The 1st phase consists of an consent with villagers and government authorities for their approval.
